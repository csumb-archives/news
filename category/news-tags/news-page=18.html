Math to the third power

The Salinas Valley is doing its part to reduce the shortage of math teachers in the U.S. Three local young women are headed to graduate school and then to the nearest classroom. The trio will be among the more than 1,300 students on track to graduate from CSU Monterey Bay on May 18.
– Salinas Californian, May 16, 2013
