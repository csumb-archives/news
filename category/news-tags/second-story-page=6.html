Capping off college with senior projects

CSUMB holds annual Capstone Festival Dec. 20 and 21

California State University, Monterey Bay’s fall Capstone Festival will be held Dec. 20 and 21 at locations around campus. The public is invited.

In order to graduate from CSUMB, all students are required to do capstones – research or creative projects. The projects require students to pull together, synthesize and apply years of learning. As part of the process, students must publicly present their work.
