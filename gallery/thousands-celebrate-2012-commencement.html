Thousands celebrate 2012 commencement

  • Media
  • Speakers Bureau
  • Magazine
  • Emergency
  • Social Media

Dr. Alice Rivlin, an economist, member of President Obama's National Commission on Fiscal Responsibility and Reform, and a senior fellow at the Brookings Institution, was the keynote speaker at CSUMB's commencement on May 19

With more than 1,120 students receiving bachelor’s and master’s degrees, it was the largest graduating class in school history. They received their diplomas before a capacity crowd of about 8,000 family members and friends.
