--- layout: archive title: Housing staff – CSUMB developing more campus housing for faculty date: January 27 2006 ---
Posted on Jan 27, 2006
As part of is planned expansion, CSUMB is gearing up to build a second phase of faculty housing. The plan calls for about 490 single-family homes, bungalows, townhomes and apartments on the northern part of campus.
– Salinas Californian, Jan. 27, 2006
