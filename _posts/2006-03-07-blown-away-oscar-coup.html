--- layout: archive title: Blown Away by Oscar Coup date: March 7 2006 ---
Posted on Mar 07, 2006
David Kashevaroff is batting 1.000 at awards shows these days. In his first appearance at the Academy Awards, Kashevaroff, a 2000 graduate of CSU-Monterey Bay's Teledramatic Arts and Technology program, brought home the big one. The documentary, "A Note of Triumph: The Golden Age of Norman Corwin," took the Oscar for best documentary short at Sunday's ceremony in Los Angeles.
– Monterey County Herald, March 7, 2006
