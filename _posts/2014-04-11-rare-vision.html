Posted on Apr 11, 2014

Kevin Miller, a veteran of CSUMB's IT Department, has figured out a way to make the Internet more accessible to the blind. It's a complicated process, but essentially, Miller's programs can help the blind and vision-impaired "see" pictures and icons by making sure images are properly tagged and can speak to a user.
– Monterey County Weekly, April 3, 2014
