--- layout: archive title: Comedy pays for college date: March 23 2006 ---
Posted on Mar 23, 2006
The problem with student fund-raisers is that no matter how noble the cause, car washes and bake sales can only get you so far. Jesus Ruvalcaba, an outreach specialist with CSUMB's Educational Talent Search who has worked to get high school students into college, came up with the idea for a comedy show but wasn't sure how to proceed. He contacted comedians online; they agreed to help. So did the Associated Students, who offered to fund the concert. A bona-fide student-run event came to life.
– Salinas Californian, March 23, 2006
