Posted on Jul 07, 2005
In a corner of the former Fort Ord, on an old field once used by the U.S .Army, the most recent and potentially most important chapter of Monterey's baseball history is being scratched out. With a rake. New CSU-Monterey Bay head baseball coach, former minor leaguer, and Monterey native Rich Aldrete smiles as he watches the piles of freshly cut two-foot weeds grow in center field.
– Monterey County Weekly, July 7, 2005
