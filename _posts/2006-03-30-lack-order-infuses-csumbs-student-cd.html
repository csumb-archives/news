--- layout: archive title: Lack of 'order' infuses CSUMB's student CD date: March 30 2006 ---
Posted on Mar 30, 2006
Naming a CD that showcases genres such as reggae, rock and hip-hop requires plenty of brainstorming. Or, just a random thought. "It came about when I was moving apartments," said Sara Bailey, a 22-year-old student at CSUMB. Bailey is producing a disc featuring 20 local bands, the majority of them university-affiliated.
– Salinas Californian, March 30, 2006
