--- layout: archive title: Student creates new 'feminist art movement' date: September 11 2014 ---
Posted on Sep 11, 2014

A handful of CSU Monterey Bay college students are aiming to produce a creative outlet for women to let their voices be heard. That platform is the Not a Pretty Girl Project, founded by Selena Gonzalez, a human communications major with an emphasis in social action creative writing.
– USA Today, Aug. 23, 2014
