Posted on Oct 19, 2006
It's hard to turn on the television without being inundated by reality TV shows. Michelle Riel, director of the Teledramatic Arts & Technology Department at CSUMB, says dozens of the program's graduates have gone on to work on reality shows such as "Project Runway," "Blind Date" and "Nanny 911."
– Salinas Californian, Oct. 19, 2006
