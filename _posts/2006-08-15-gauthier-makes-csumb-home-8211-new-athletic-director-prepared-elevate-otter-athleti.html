--- layout: archive title: Gauthier makes CSUMB home – New athletic director prepared to elevate Otter athletics date: August 15 2006 ---
Posted on Aug 15, 2006
Glancing out the windows of California State University, Monterey Bay's Alumni & Visitors Center, Howard Gauthier signed at the familiarity. "I grew up in a small town that looks quite a bit like the Monterey Bay area," said Gauthier, who was named the school's director of athletics. "So there's a high level of comfort in being here." Gauthier most recently served as the athletic director at Division II Southwest Minnesota State University.
– Salinas Californian, Aug. 15, 2006
