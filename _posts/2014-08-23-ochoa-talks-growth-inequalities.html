--- layout: archive title: Ochoa talks of growth, inequalities date: August 23 2014 ---
Posted on Aug 23, 2014

CSU Monterey Bay President Eduardo Ochoa launched the 2014-15 academic year with a 45-minute speech that touched on the university's recent accomplishments, its restructuring, immediate and long-term plans, national education policy, the growing inequality gap, the university's developing role in shaping Monterey Bay and California's drought.
– Monterey Herald, Aug. 22, 2014
