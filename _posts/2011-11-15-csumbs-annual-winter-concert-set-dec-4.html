Posted on Nov 15, 2011

Community invited to share the season with students in the music program

CSU Monterey Bay’s Music and Performing Arts Department invites the community to attend its annual winter concert at 3 p.m., Dec. 4, in the World Theater on Sixth Avenue.

The university’s gospel choir and small vocal ensemble, Nuovo Plaisir – both directed by Professor Paulette Gissendanner – will perform. Also on the program is the CSUMB concert band, directed by Theresa Hruby-Percell, and a jazz/blues ensemble directed by Professor Richard Bains, chair of the Music and Performing Arts Department.

The event is free; no tickets or reservations are required. Free parking is available in the lot adjacent to the theater.
