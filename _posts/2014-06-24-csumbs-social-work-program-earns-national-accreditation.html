--- layout: archive title: CSUMB's social work program earns national accreditation date: June 24 2014 ---
Posted on Jun 24, 2014

CSU Monterey Bay's master's degree in social work is now nationally accredited. The Council of Social Work Education has spent four years examining the program. Evaluators recently issued their report, granting initial accreditation through February 2018.
– Monterey Herald, June 22, 2014
