--- layout: archive title: CSUMB lauded for service – university receives national award for helping communities date: October 17 2006 ---
Posted on Oct 17, 2006
One of the academic hallmarks of CSMB received antional recognition from the federal government's pre-eminent volunteer agency. The university is one of three to be placed on the President's Higher Education Community Service Honor Roll for community service, thanks to the school's service-learning curriculum.
– Salinas Californian, Oct. 17, 2006
