--- layout: archive title: Panetta has big plans for institute date: July 15 2013 ---
Posted on Jul 15, 2013

Panetta said the institute will expand, and that he and Sylvia are in the midst of trying to secure a spot on campus to construct a new building to host the popular Panetta Lecture Series – which draws political leaders and policy thinkers to the Peninsula to talk about national and international issues.
– Carmel Pine Cone, July 12, 2013
