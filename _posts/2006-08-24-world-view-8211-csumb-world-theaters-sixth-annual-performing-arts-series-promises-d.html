--- layout: archive title: World view – CSUMB World Theater's sixth annual performing arts series promises to deliver the world date: August 24 2006 ---
Posted on Aug 24, 2006
The theme of the World Theater's Performing Arts Series, set to begin Sept. 21 on the CSU-Monterey Bay campus is deceiving. "Welcome to the World!" it calls out from this aptly named performance center, but introducing international cutting-edge artists is exactly what it has done for all of its short existence.
– Monterey County Herald, Aug. 24, 2006
