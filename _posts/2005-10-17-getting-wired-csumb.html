--- layout: archive title: Getting wired at CSUMB date: October 17 2005 ---
Posted on Oct 17, 2005
While a recent survey published by Intel Corp. ranked CSU-Monterey Bay 12th among the top 50 most unwired U.S. campuses, the college has its own response to that. "We have 20 projects with 28 faculty that will leave you flabbergasted," said Arlene Krebs, founding director of the school's Wireless Education and Technology Center.
– Monterey County Herald, Oct. 17, 2005
