Preview and Admissions Day Nov. 12 at CSUMB

Have you wondered what life is like at California State University, Monterey Bay?

Prospective students and their parents will have an opportunity to find out at Preview and Admissions Day on Saturday, Nov. 12. The event will be held in University Center, Building 29, on Sixth Avenue. Parking fee will be waived.

Information will be available on:

  • Freshman housing
  • Financial aid
  • Campus life
  • Academic programs

Prospective students can also take a campus tour, visit campus housing and attend an information fair.

Check-in will start at 8:30 a.m.; the program will begin at 9:30; workshops and tours will take place from 9:30 to 2 p.m. Anyone who plans to attend is asked to RSVP by calling 582-3738 by Friday, Nov. 4.

Applications are being accepted for spring 2006 as well as next fall for freshmen and transfer students. Students who have submitted an application and supporting documents by Nov. 4 can learn their admissions results at the event. Applications can be submitted online at csumentor.edu (application fee payable online).

CSUMB works to make events accessible to all. If you have special needs, please call 582-3738.
