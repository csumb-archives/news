--- layout: archive title: Films cap college careers – CSUMB festival to showcase student projects date: December 16 2006 ---
Posted on Dec 16, 2006
A high school girl yearnedto attend college and to shape her future. "No," her mother said. "If you go away to college, you'll never come back to the family." This idea of a generational clash sparked the beginning of Juan Figueroa's film "Lettuce Roots." The upshot is a 5.5-minute film, one of 14 by CSUMB students to be shown at the Capstone Festival on Wednesday.
– The Salinas Californian, Dec. 16, 2006
