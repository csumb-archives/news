--- layout: archive title: Devastating Images – CSUMB student's Katrina documentary to be shown at Black History event date: February 13 2006 ---
Posted on Feb 13, 2006
Filmmaker Estee Blancher said she wants viewers to be moved to tears – or at the very least be awestruck – by the images of destruction she captured in New Orleans. Overturned cars, flattened homes and people rebuilding neighborhoods are some of the images Blancher captured on her visit to the areas affected by Hurricane Katrina six months ago.
– Monterey County Herald, Feb. 13, 2006
