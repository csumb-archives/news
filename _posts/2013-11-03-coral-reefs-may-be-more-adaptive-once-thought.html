--- layout: archive title: Coral reefs may be more adaptive than once thought date: November 3 2013 ---
Posted on Nov 03, 2013

Scientists studying the catastrophic phenomena of coral bleaching have concluded that reef systems may be more adaptable to increasingly warmer oceans than previously believed. The work was funded by NOAA and conducted by researchers at Cal State Monterey Bay and the University of British Columbia.
– Los Angeles Times science blog, Oct. 29, 2013
