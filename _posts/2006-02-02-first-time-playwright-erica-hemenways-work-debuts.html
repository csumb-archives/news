--- layout: archive title: Firsttime playwright Erica Hemenway's work debuts date: February 2 2006 ---
Posted on Feb 02, 2006
Bringing a character to life on stage is complex in iself. Taking five different characters from five different stories and five different time periods, well, that's just crazy talk. But Pacific Grove playwright Erica Hemenway just doesn't know any better. "I started doing it as a character study," explained Hemenway, a 25-year-old CSUMB graduate who received a degree from the school's Teledramatic Arts and Technology program.
– Monterey County Herald, Feb. 2, 2006
