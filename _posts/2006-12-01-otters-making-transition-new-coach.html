--- layout: archive title: Otters making transition to new coach date: December 1 2006 ---
Posted on Dec 01, 2006
After one game, it's impossible to evaluate how effective Pat Kosta will be as CSU-Monterey Bay's new men's basketball coach. One thing is for sure – the transition should be easy. Kosta spent six seasons as an assistant coach under Bill Trumbo, who left the school in June.
– Monterey Herald, Dec. 1, 2006
