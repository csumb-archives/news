--- layout: archive title: Reflections of Vietnam – Panel to discuss California's role in the war date: November 15 2006 ---
Posted on Nov 15, 2006
Two visions of Vietnam followed David Anderson, long after he left that land in 1970. "First, the beauty of Vietnam," said Anderson, an Army veteran. "Green rice fields almost to the water's edge. White sand beaches. The people so very open and friendly." Then the war itself . . . "The trauma and sacrifice of it. It nags at people," said Anderson, dean of California State University, Monterey Bay's College of University Studies and Programs.
– The Salinas Californian, Nov. 15, 2006
