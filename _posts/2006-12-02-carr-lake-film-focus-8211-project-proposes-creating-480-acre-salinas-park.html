--- layout: archive title: Carr Lake film focus – project proposes creating 480acre Salinas park date: December 2 2006 ---
Posted on Dec 02, 2006
A new short documentary telling the history of Crr Lake and its potential to become a regional park in the center of Salinas, which would be larger than New York's Central Park, debuted Friday in Salinas. The film, "Carr Lake, Cultivating Community," was produced and made by students from CSUMB.
– The Salinas Californian, Dec.2, 2006
