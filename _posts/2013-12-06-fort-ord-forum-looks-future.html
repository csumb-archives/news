--- layout: archive title: Fort Ord forum looks to future date: December 6 2013 ---
Posted on Dec 06, 2013

As last month's election battle over growth on part of Fort Ord showed, the debate over the future of the former Army base is far from over. To look at the various issues from several angles, CSU Monterey Bay and the Fort Ord Reuse Authority are sponsoring a two-day gathering of experts aimed at the public and local decision-makers.
– Monterey Herald, Dec. 6, 2013
