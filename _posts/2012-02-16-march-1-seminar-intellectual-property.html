Are you ready to patent your idea?

On March 1, the Small Business Development Center at CSU Monterey Bay and several other organzations will present a free seminar on intellectual property protection. The seminar will be held from 1 to 4 p.m. at the Marina library, 190 Seaside Circle, Marina.

The free seminar will cover the four types of intellectual property – patents, copyrights, trademarks and trade secrets – as well as the requirements for patentability. It will also provide an overview of the patent process and the role of intellectual property in fundraising.

Presenters include:

• Grace Liu, patent attorney and intellectual property specialist at Fresno State
• Robert Chamberlain, president of Monterey Technologies, Inc.
• Robert Mykland, CEO of Ascenium
• Patrick Reilly, patent attorney and founder of the Intellectual Property Society

Pre-registration is required and can be done online at http://bit.ly/sbdcworkshops, or contact Veronica Munoz at 675-7232.

Other sponsors include the Institute for Innovation and Economic Development, Monterey County Business Council, the Central Coast SBDC, Project 17 Agricultural Technology Cluster and the Marina Technology Cluster.
