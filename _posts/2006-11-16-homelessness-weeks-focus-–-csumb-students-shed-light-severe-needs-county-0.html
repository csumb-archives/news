--- layout: archive title: Homelessness week's focus – CSUMB students shed light on severe needs in county date: November 16 2006 ---
Posted on Nov 16, 2006
Students at California State University, Monterey Bay have been holding events this week as part of National Hunger and Homelessness Awareness Week. The activities are sponsored by the Student Leadership in Service Learning group and are intended to bring attention to severe needs among low-income and homeless populations in Monterey County. The theme of the week is "One Paycheck Away," meaning that a significant segment of the workforce is one paycheck away from losing their homes.
– The Salinas Californian, Nov. 16, 2006
