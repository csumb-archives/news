Cal State-Monterey Bay catcher Alex Swartz says the Otters baseball team is "old school," but the jargon sounds odd. The school is fielding a team for the first time in school history. It's home diamond, along with the rest of campus, resides on the former Fort Ord, a long-time military base selected for decommissioning in 1991.
– Santa Cruz Sentinel, Feb. 3, 2006
