--- layout: archive title: Students learn value of service date: October 8 2005 ---
Posted on Oct 08, 2005
Nearly 200 high school students gathered at California State University, Monterey Bay, to learn about teamwork and community involvement at an innovative new service fair. CSUMB, which incorporates service learning into its own program, is helping connect students with volunteer opportunities while gaining a greater understanding of service itself.
– Salinas Californian, Oct. 8, 2005
