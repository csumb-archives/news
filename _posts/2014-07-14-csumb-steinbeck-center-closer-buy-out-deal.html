--- layout: archive title: CSUMB, Steinbeck Center closer to buyout deal date: July 14 2014 ---
Posted on Jul 14, 2014

Salinas city officials have agreed to forgive the National Steinbeck Center nearly $500,000 in deferred loan payments as part of a bid by CSU Monterey Bay's University Corporation to purchase the downtown center's building and land, and help save the financially struggling organization.
– Monterey Herald, July 10, 2014
