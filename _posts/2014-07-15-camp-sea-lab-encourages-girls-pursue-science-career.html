--- layout: archive title: Camp SEA Lab encourages girls to pursue science career date: July 15 2014 ---
Posted on Jul 15, 2014

Twenty-four high school students from around the country got some cool hands-on training in Monterey Bay on Monday, exploring the world of marine science. Camp SEA Lab summer camp is put on by CSU Monterey Bay; it encourages teenage girls to pursue careers in science.
– KION-TV, July 14, 2014
