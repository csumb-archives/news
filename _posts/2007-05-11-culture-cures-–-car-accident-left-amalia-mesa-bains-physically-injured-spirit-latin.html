--- layout: archive title: Culture Cures – a car accident left Amalia MesaBains physically injured but the spirit of the Latina artist thrives date: May 11 2007 ---
Posted on May 11, 2007
La Cultura Cura - The culture cures. Amalia Mesa-Bains used that simple mantra as her strength, her personal miracle, while recovering from severe injuries suffered in a 2003 car wreck. In her case, la cultura wasn't just the cure, it propelled the healing process. "I do believe that healing is not necessarily a physical process," said Mesa-Bains, a professor at CSU-Monterey Bay. "It's a process that involves family, spirituality . . . and cultura."
– Monterey Herald, May 11, 2007
