--- layout: archive title: Films track troubles in Mexico date: December 5 2006 ---
Posted on Dec 05, 2006
A CSU-Monterey Bay professor who spent the summer in Mexico City has produced a 30-minute documentary film about the chaotic political situation in Mexico. Caitlin Manning, a professor of film and video, was in Mexico when the populace took to the streets after the ruling party's candidate, Felipe Calderon, squeaked to a victory with the slimmest of margins.
– Monterey Herald, Dec. 5, 2006
