--- layout: archive title: Educational programs advance the local tourism industry date: September 29 2014 ---
Posted on Sep 29, 2014

Soon, CSUMB will introduce one of the country's first sustainable hospitality management programs. Students who earn the bachelor's degree, which will offer concentrations in sustainability and ecotourism, sustainable resort and hotel management, and sustainable event planning and management, will be equipped to build and lead eco-friendly tourism businesses.
– Monterey Herald, Sept. 12, 2014
