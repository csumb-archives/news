--- layout: archive title: Obstacles lead man to Hearst award date: November 12 2005 ---
Posted on Nov 12, 2005
Daniel Delacruz, 37, didn't think much of life beyond driving a forklift when he worked for a Salinas agriculture company more than 15 years ago. The CSU-Monterey Bay senior dropped out of Alisal High School as a senior and was several credits short of a high school diploma. But a life-changing illness made him re-think his direction.
– Monterey County Herald, Nov. 12, 2005
