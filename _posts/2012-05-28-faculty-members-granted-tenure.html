--- layout: archive title: Faculty members granted tenure date: May 28 2012 ---
Posted on May 28, 2012

President Dianne Harrison has announced that seven faculty members at California State University, Monterey Bay, have been granted tenure.

The newly tenured faculty members and their departments:

• Mr. Kevin Cahill, School of Information Technology and Communication Design
• Dr. Rachel Esselstein, Mathematics and Statistics Department
• Dr. Miguel Lopez, Liberal Studies Department
• Dr. Bude Su, School of Information Technology and Communication Design
• Dr. Umi Vaughan, Division of Humanities and Communication
• Dr. Maria Villaseñor, Division of Humanities and Communication
• Dr. Fred Watson, Division of Science and Environmental Policy

Along with to receiving tenure, all were promoted to associate professor.

In addition, Dr. Juan Jose Gutierrez, Division of Social, Behavioral and Global Studies; Dr. James Raines, Department of Health, Human Services and Public Policy; and Dr. Dan Shapiro, Division of Science and Environmental Policy, were promoted to full professor.
