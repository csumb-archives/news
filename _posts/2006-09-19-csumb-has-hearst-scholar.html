--- layout: archive title: CSUMB has a Hearst scholar date: September 19 2006 ---
Posted on Sep 19, 2006
If all's going as planned, California State University, Monterey Bay senior Adriana Pacheco is driving this morning to Long Beach to pick up a $3,000 check. Pacheco, 22, will be honored as a recipient of a William Randolph Hearst/CSU Trustees' Scholarship at the CSU chancellor's office. The award, one of 19 given in the 405,000-student system, goes to students who demonstrate academic success while overcoming socioeconomic hardship.
– Salinas Californian, Sept. 19, 2006
