--- layout: archive title: Earthbound Farm goes to college via agreement with Sodexho date: September 9 2005 ---
Posted on Sep 09, 2005
San Juan Bautista-based Earthbound Farm has partnered with California State University, Montery Bay and food service provider Sodexho to provide organic produce for the university's cafeteria year round. Credited with joining the parties together is Kari Bernardi, project coordinator for the Monterey County Farm to School Partnership, which is the local chapter of a national movement that links schools and farms.
– The Produce News, Sept. 9, 2005
