--- layout: archive title: Report on 'California's Ocean Economy' date: August 11 2005 ---
Posted on Aug 11, 2005
"California's Ocean Economy," a 156-page report highlighting the importance of the ocean and coast to California's economy, was released July 22 by the Resources Agency at the first California Ocean and Coastal Economic Summit. The report was written by Dr. Judith Kildow of CSUMB and Dr. Charles Colgan of the University of Southern Maine.
– Mendocino Beacon, Aug. 11, 2005
