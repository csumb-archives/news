--- layout: archive title: CSUMB clicks on its book wants date: September 23 2005 ---
Posted on Sep 23, 2005
The librarians at California State University, Monterey Bay have found an innovative way to build the school's collection. It's called Amazon.com. The popular online retailer offers "Wish List" gift registries, which allow users to view items a customer would like to receive. The school's alumni relations team saw this as the perfect opportunity to enlist graduates' support for the new Tanimura & Antle Family Memorial Library.
– Salinas Californian, Sept. 23, 2005
