--- layout: archive title: Museum will tell story of Fort Ord date: August 1 2005 ---
Posted on Aug 01, 2005
Things have been piling up in Steven Levinson's garage. It's full of memorabilia such as uniforms, phone books and photographs from the former U.S. Army base at Fort Ord. "We're hoping to have a museum dedicated to Fort Ord, which has had an important role in the development of Monterey County," said Levinson, co-director of the archives and a Teledramatic Arts and Technology professor.
–Salinas Californian, Aug. 1, 2005
