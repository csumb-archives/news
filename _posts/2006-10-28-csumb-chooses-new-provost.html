--- layout: archive title: CSUMB chooses new provost date: October 28 2006 ---
Posted on Oct 28, 2006
Under the tutelage of an adviser at the University of Chicago, Kathryn Cruz-Uribe learned that animal bones are fascinating. "Learning about human behavior and bones is fun," Cruz-Uribe said via cell phone from Flagstaff, Ariz., where she serves as the dean of the College of Social and Behavioral Sciences at Northern Arizona University. In the coming months, she will trade her responsibilities in Flagstaff for the position of provost and vice president of academic affairs at CSU-Monterey Bay.
– Monterey County Herald, Oct. 28, 2006
