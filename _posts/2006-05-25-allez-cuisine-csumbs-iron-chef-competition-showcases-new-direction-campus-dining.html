--- layout: archive title: Allez cuisine! CSUMB's 'Iron Chef' competition showcases the new direction of campus dining date: May 25 2006 ---
Posted on May 25, 2006
The memories are clouded by time and dead brain cells, but I remember my college dining hall as a belly-busting shirine to lard and carbs. The highlight of the week came Friday when we choked down a gristly steak and mounds of mac 'n' cheese. In stark contrast is the dining experience at California State University, Montery Bay, where students dine on oganic salad greens and fruit, lemon-herb chicken with sun-dried tomato basil couscous or Monterey Bay salmon with Cajun spices and lemon caper aioli.
– Monterey County Herald, May 25, 2006
