--- layout: archive title: A. Lee – Music writer talks to a CSUMB hiphop artist about her debut CD and how she hooked up with virgin label Fog Box date: December 14 2006 ---
Posted on Dec 14, 2006
You may already be familiar with Fog Box record label from its founder's two compilation CD releases, "Unexploded Ordinance" and "Out of Order," featuring local acts from the California State University, Monterey Bay campus, and beyond. This week marks another first for the label when hip-hop artist A.Lee celebrates the release of her debut CVD, "The Channel." "This is our first full-length CD by a single artist," said Fog Box founder and CSUMB student Sara Bailey.
– The Salinas Californian, Dec. 14, 2006
