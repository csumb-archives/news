--- layout: archive title: Students spruce up park – Effort benefits environment, community date: August 29 2006 ---
Posted on Aug 29, 2006
Armed with spray paint, plastic gloves and garbage bags, about 75 new students from California State University, Monterey Bay descended on Natividad Creek Park in north Salinas on Saturday. Their goal? Remove some of the hundreds of pounds of litter in and around the park while educating parkgoers and neighborhood residents about the creek's role in ocean health.
– Salinas Californian, Aug. 29, 2006
