--- layout: archive title: A brighter future – First generation students aim high date: March 7 2006 ---
Posted on Mar 07, 2006
For teens whose parents didn't attend college, higher education is uncharted territory: confusing, frightening and seemingly out of reach. But the glittery promises of better jobs beckon, and some first-generation students find ways to make it happen – with help from the Educational Talent Search program based at Cal State-Monterey Bay.
– Salinas Californian, March 7, 2006
