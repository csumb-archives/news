--- layout: archive title: Otter men win own invitational date: October 25 2013 ---
Posted on Oct 25, 2013

The No. 8-ranked Otters cruised to a win in their own Otter Invitational at par-72 Black Horse Golf Course, finishing at 10-under-par and 27 strokes ahead of runner-up and No. 1-ranked Barry University of Miami.
– Monterey Herald, Oct. 23, 2013
