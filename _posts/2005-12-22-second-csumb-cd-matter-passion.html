--- layout: archive title: Second CSUMB CD a matter of passion date: December 22 2005 ---
Posted on Dec 22, 2005
For the second year in a row, Sara Bailey, a student at CSUMB, is producing a CD featuring 20 bands and musicians. Most of them are students and alumni. Bailey hopes to release the free CD to students in the spring. Her longtime goal – and senior capstone project – is to launch a CSUMB-based label that would record and promote musicians in the CSU system.
– Salinas Californian, Dec. 22, 2005
