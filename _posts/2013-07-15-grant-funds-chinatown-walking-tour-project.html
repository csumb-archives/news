--- layout: archive title: Grant funds Chinatown walking tour project date: July 15 2013 ---
Posted on Jul 15, 2013

For six years, Rina Benmayor's students at CSU Monterey Bay have worked to preserve memories of Chinatown in Salinas. That work has received a $10,000 boost from Cal Humanities toward a project titled "Imagine Salinas Chinatown: An Oral History Walking Tour."
– The Salinas Californian, July 15, 2013
