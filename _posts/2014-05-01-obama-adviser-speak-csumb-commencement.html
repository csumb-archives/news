Posted on May 01, 2014

The point person to President Obama on immigration reform will deliver the keynote address at this year's commencement ceremony at CSU Monterey Bay. Cecilia Muñoz, the highest-ranking Latina in the White House, is director of the Domestic Policy Council.
– The Salinas Californian, April 30, 2014
