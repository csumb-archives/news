--- layout: archive title: On a Mission – An archaeology professor explores secrets behind local historic churches date: August 3 2006 ---
Posted on Aug 03, 2006
It's a dreary winter solstice morning. Suddenly, the fog blanket splits and sunlight shoots through. It travels through the window above the front door of a dimly lit Spanish colonial church, its beam of reddish amber light hitting the back wall. Suddenly, the entire interior is enrobed with light. It's not a scene from an Indiana Jones movie, though with a few snakes and a bullwhip, it could be.
– Monterey County Weekly, Aug. 3, 2006
